Introduction

Sound Buddies ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application.

Sound Buddies does not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. We do not track your usage, collect analytics, or gather any data about you or your device.

All data created or used within the app is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.
